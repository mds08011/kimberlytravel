ORP Reference: If your controller reads ORP, ensure it is within acceptable ranges. If ORP is low but PPM is high, check your Cyanuric Acid or pH!
Rule of Thumb: Always fix Alkalinity BEFORE attempting to adjust pH. We use Sodium Bicarbonate (Baking Soda) to raise Alkalinity.
Note on CO2: CO2 automatically lowers pH without adding total dissolved solids. If your CO2 is running but pH isn't dropping, check if the cylinder is empty or if Alkalinity is too high.
